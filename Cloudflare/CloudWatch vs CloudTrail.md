
[[CloudTrail Core]]

|**Feature**|**AWS CloudWatch**|**AWS CloudTrail**|
|---|---|---|
|**Focus**|Performance & Health ("How are my resources doing?").|Activity & Auditing ("Who touched my resources?").|
|**Data Types**|Metrics, Logs, Alarms.|API Call History (Events).|
|**Example**|"CPU on **ISG-PROVIDER** is 90%.".|"User 'Admin' deleted the **RDS** instance.".|
|**Trigger**|Time-based or threshold-based.|Event-based (API call).|
